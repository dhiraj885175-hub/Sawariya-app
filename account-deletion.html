🗑️ Account Delete Request

Agar aap apna Sawariya account delete karna chahte hain, to neeche diye gaye steps follow karein.

📋 Steps to Delete Account

  1. 1 Apna registered mobile number ya email taiyar rakhen.
  2. 2 Neeche diye gaye WhatsApp ya Email button par click karen.
  3. 3 Message mein likhen: "Mujhe apna account delete karna hai" aur apna number bhejein.
  4. 4 Hamari team 24-48 ghante mein aapka account delete kar degi.
💬 WhatsApp par Request Bhejein 📧 Email par Request Bhejein

🗂️ Kaun sa Data Delete Hoga?

  • ✅ Profile information (naam, photo, number)
  • ✅ Order history
  • ✅ Saved addresses
  • ✅ Payment details
  • ✅ All personal data

⚠️ Account delete hone ke baad data recover nahi kiya ja sakta.
