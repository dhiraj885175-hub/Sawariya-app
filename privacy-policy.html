👗 Sawariya — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Sawariya ("hum", "humara app") ek local kapda (clothing) store ka mobile application hai jo Motipur, Bihar mein operate hota hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum app use karte waqt aapki kya jaankari collect karte hain, kaise use karte hain, aur aap apne data par kya control rakhte hain.

1. Hum kya jaankari collect karte hain

  • Account jaankari: Naam, email address, phone number (jab aap account banate ho ya login karte ho)
  • Order jaankari: Aapke order, delivery address, payment method (COD/UPI), order photos
  • Device/usage jaankari: App ka basic usage data, taaki app behtar bana sakein
  • Guest users: Agar aap Guest ke roop mein app use karte ho, to hum permanent account nahi banate, sirf temporary session data store hota hai

2. Hum is jaankari ka use kaise karte hain

  • Aapka order process karne aur deliver karne ke liye
  • Aapse order/delivery se related communication ke liye (chat feature ke through)
  • App ko behtar banane aur naye offers dikhane ke liye
  • Hum aapki jaankari kisi third-party advertiser ko bechte nahi hain

3. Data kahan store hota hai

Aapka data Google Firebase (Firestore) par securely store hota hai, jo Google ki cloud infrastructure use karta hai. Login ke liye hum Firebase Authentication use karte hain (Email/Password, Google Sign-In, ya Guest/Anonymous login).

4. Aapke rights — Data Access aur Deletion

Aap kabhi bhi apna account aur usse juda data delete karne ki request kar sakte ho:

  • App ke andar: Home screen ke ⚙️ icon se "Account Delete Karo" option use karke
  • App ke bahar: is page par visit karke request bhej sakte ho

Delete request milne par hum aapka account aur personal jaankari (naam, email, phone) hata dete hain. Kanooni/accounting zaroorat ke liye purane order records ka basic transaction history dukaan ke paas rakha ja sakta hai, jisme aapki personal pehchaan hata di jaati hai.

5. Permissions jo app maangta hai

  • Camera/Gallery: Order ke liye photo upload karne ke liye (sirf jab aap khud photo chuno)
  • Internet: App ko Firebase server se data laane/bhejne ke liye

6. Children ki Privacy

Ye app 13 saal se kam umar ke bachchon ko target nahi karta. Hum jaan-bujhkar bachchon se data collect nahi karte.

7. Policy mein badlaav

Hum is Privacy Policy ko samay-samay par update kar sakte hain. Koi bada badlaav hone par hum app mein notify karenge.
